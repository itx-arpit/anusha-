but stayed silent.

but because I didn't know if it was the right moment.

but some moments stay with us for a long time.

Sometimes a simple conversation can stay in someone's

thoughts longer than expected.

There was a moment when I realised something

had slowly started changing inside me.

I began noticing when you were around,

and those moments felt different.

I wondered whether I should say anything

about these thoughts or not.

Sometimes the hardest thing is deciding

whether to stay silent or speak honestly.

But ignoring feelings doesn't make them disappear.

They simply stay somewhere quietly

waiting to be understood.

So I decided it is better to say things honestly once

instead of always wondering what could have happened

if I never said anything.

Just so you could read it once.
